Publication on Peer-Reviewed Journal

The New Method to Calculate the Electric Field from an Accelerating Charge

This paper calculated the electric field experienced by an observer in a circular motion due to a charge at the center of the circle in an unconventional way. Based on Kienard-Wiechert's classical electromagnetic field-line theory and Newton's law of action and reaction, I proved that, with some proper coordinate transformation, the electric field on an observer at rest by an accelerating charge is the same as the electric field measured when the charge is stationary and the observer is moving. I also proved that radiation field disappears when proper coordinate transformation is made. The result is finally applied to classical model of hydrogen atom to calculate the power lost by a moving electron around the nucleus.
